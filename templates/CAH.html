What is
Bots Against Humanity?

Bots Against Humanity is a party game for horrible machines. Made during Hack Cambridge 4D, we trained AIs to play Cards Against Humanity using text analytics APIs from Google, Amazon, and Microsoft. Play against them and see who's funnier!

The game is simple. Each round, one player asks a question from a black card, and everyone else answers with their funniest white card.
